Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shipping cost for bike orders?

We offer free standard shipping on all bike orders within the continental United States. For international orders or expedited shipping options, additional charges may apply, which will be calculated at checkout.

What is the return policy if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for all bike purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with your bike, you can return it within 30 days from the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Please note that the bike must be in its original condition and packaging for a successful return.

Are the bikes covered by insurance during shipping?

Yes, all bikes shipped from our warehouse are insured against damage and loss during transit. In the rare event of any damage during shipping, please contact our customer support team immediately with relevant photos and details, and we'll work to resolve the issue promptly.

How can I track the status of my bike order?

Once your order is processed and shipped, we'll provide you with a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number to monitor the real-time status of your bike delivery. Simply click on the provided tracking link or visit our website's "Order Tracking" page and enter your tracking number to get the latest updates on your shipment.
